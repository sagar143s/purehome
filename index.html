Our Info

AL KHALIDIYA

In its position as the capital of the UAE, Abu Dhabi boasts a great variety of properties for sale to meet the growing demand for quality, affordable and stylish housing in the city. As the largest Emirate, it has a lot of room for expansion and the local property market has seen small but steady growth in recent times.

AL MUSHRIF

Masdar City is the world’s first zero-carbon green metropolis plan being developed in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Masdar City is a planned city project in Abu Dhabi, in the United Arab Emirates. Its core is being built by Masdar.

AL REEM ISLAND

Al Reem Island is one of the fastest-developing neighbourhoods in Abu Dhabi. Reem Island is a natural island 600 metres off the coast of Abu Dhabi island. Located off the northeastern coast of Abu Dhabi, it is a mixed purpose community with both residential, retail and commercial units.
